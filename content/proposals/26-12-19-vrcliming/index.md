## Immersive Climbing Game Project

This project features an **immersive climbing game** designed specifically for hosting **climbing championships among students**. Built in Godot with VR/XR support, it recreates realistic rock climbing walls, bouldering routes, and competitive formats where players grip holds, plan routes, and compete in real-time.

### Key Features
- **Realistic climbing mechanics**: Hand-tracking VR interactions with dynamic grip strength, foot placement, and fatigue simulation to mimic real climbing physics.
- **Modular climbing walls**: Procedurally generated or hand-crafted routes (beginner V0 to advanced V8 grades) that students can design and share.
- **Championship mode**: Tournament brackets with live leaderboards, spectator camera views, and timed ascents or flash/on-sight scoring.
- **Multiplayer & social**: Up to 8 players competing simultaneously, with classroom leaderboards and replay analysis for technique review.

### Educational Value
Students first **learn to develop** the climbing mechanics, route generation algorithms, and multiplayer networking. Then they **playtest and compete** using their creations, turning the classroom into a competitive arena. This closes the "create-play-share-compete" lifecycle, boosting engagement through ownership and friendly rivalry.

Perfect as a TFG project or extended practice module combining game design, physics simulation, VR UX, and competitive multiplayer systems.
